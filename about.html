For thousands of years, we've been quietly (and sometimes very loudly) raising dragons of every shape, size, and temperament imaginable.
From pocket-sized emberlings to rare, sky darkening ancients, our dragons are known for their exceptional health, gentle training, and legendary lineage.
We believe every dragon deserves a good home, and every dragon keeper deserves the perfect match.
That's why we obsess over care, ethics, and preservation, ensuring each dragon is raised with respect, patience, and just the right amount of magic.
Our company is run by a devoted team of dragonkeepers, wyrm-scholars, fire-tenders, and scale-smiths, all bound by a shared love of these magnificent creatures and a few ancient oaths we legally cannot discuss.
Whether you're seeking a loyal hearth guardian, a rare celestial breed, or a lifelong winged companion, you're holding centuries of knowledge, and a little dragon fire, in your hands.
